Projects

Whispers of the Veil

Whispers of the Veil is a 2.5D pixelated horror adventure game that combines atmospheric horror with cutting-edge interactive mechanics. The game integrates voice commands for solving puzzles and interacting with the environment, supported by adaptive AI-driven gameplay to create a unique, personalized experience. Players assume the role of an older brother navigating a cursed forest to rescue his missing sister, encountering monsters, solving puzzles, and making impactful decisions that shape the narrative. This document outlines the project’s purpose, its significance, the legal and ethical aspects, any changes and progress since the initial project concept, the project's responsibilities, monitoring, and risks, as well as the accessibility concerns.

Speech Recognition Machine Learning Model

This project aims to allow you to train your own Speech Recognition ML Model and utilize it in your own projects by interacting with it via an API. Included a pre-trained model that you can utilize instead of a custom one. This pre-trained model was trained on the LibriSpeech dataset, it as a Word-Error-Rate (WER) of 28%.

Bellhop

At Hellman Hotel, we take pride in treating our guests with honor, dignity, and respect. Except for the Journalist, he’s been shot in the face. He’s been spilling so much tea in his articles that he’s had it coming. I’m the unbiased narrator and I’m glad it happened.
Anyways, the Bellhop must investigate the Boxer, the Chef, and the Politician in this escape room/murder-mystery type game. Each suite will consist of puzzles and clues that will help you uncover the truth. Bring justice to the Journalist by tracking down his murderer! Or don’t, he’s kind of a piece of shit.

Honor Of War

Based in a ward time. You are part of a military convoy transporting sensitive documents. When your fleet was set upon by enemy forces. Despite your teams best effort, the enemy is able to overpower you; causing the entire fleet to crash. Somehow you manage to survive, but you notice that the crash site has been picked clean by the enemy. You find yourself behind enemy lines, sensitive battle plans stolen, and your entire team killed. Your goal is to find your way back to the base; while collecting the correspondence that the enemy stole along the way.
